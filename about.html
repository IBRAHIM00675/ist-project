Welcome to the Institute of
Software Technologies

Institute of Software Technologies Limited was a
Department under Software Technologies Limited since
its establishment in 1991.

The Institute of Software Technologies Limited was registered on 21st November
2004. We are located on the 6th Floor of West Point building, Mpaka rd,
Westlands/Parklands opp Oshwal Primary School, Nairobi, Kenya. Our Center has
20 well-equipped modern training labs and the capacity to hold 200 students at a
time.

Transform your passion into professional success

One distinguishing feature of Institute of Software Technologies and its academic, teaching, and other personnel is our dedication to providing not only a valuable but also a pleasurable learning experience. We have a welcoming environment, and we also offer a variety of extracurricular social and other activities.
Institute of software Technologies is a fully-fledged Kenyan Institution registered with the Ministry of Higher Education, Science, and Technology (MOHEST), to provide Technical, Industrial, Vocational, and Entrepreneurship Training (TVET) Institution. No. MOHEST/PC/1406/011. We are also registred with the National Industrial Training Authority (NITA) as Training Providers.

We are Red Hat Accredited Training and the ONLY Testing Center in the region, Ec-council Accredited training and Testing Center, ATL Training and Testing Center, International Certification for Digital Literacy (ICDL) Accredited training and Testing Center. Pearson Vue Testing center, PSI testing center among other partnerships. These prestigious appointments reflect the confidence that our partners have in our ability to deliver hands-on training that is of the highest international standards.

You'll do more than just earn a Diploma/Certificate at Institute Of Software Technologies. You will discover your purpose and redefine what it means to succeed personally, professionally, and socially. Take advantage of:

  1. Accelerated timetable to help you complete your Diploma/Certificate faster.
  2. Schedules that are convenient for you, with a combination of online classes, day classes and Saturday classes.
  3. Individualized career counseling and job search assistance to help you get started with your career.
